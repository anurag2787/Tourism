Top three activity to do in Jaunpur

Sahi Pul

This famous bridge of Jaunpur was built by Munyeen Khankhana in 1564 on the orders of Akbar during his reign.

Atala Masjid

In 1408 A.D., Ibrahim Shah Sharki built the Atala Masjid which came to be considered as the ideal for the construction of the other mosques of Jaunpur.

Shahi Qila

Situated in the heart of the city on the left bank of the Gomti, Shahi Qila was built by Freoze Shah in 1362 A.D. The inner gate of this fort is 26.5 ft. high and 16 ft. wide.
